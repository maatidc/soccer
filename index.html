Our Philosophy

We are all here to enjoy the game, stay active, and respect one another. It's important to remember that most of us are balancing families, careers, and other commitments — our shared priority is to stay safe, healthy, and injury-free so we can return to our daily lives without issue. That respect must extend onto the field.

Team Rules

Please refrain from the following actions:

  • Slide tackling from behind
  • Intentional kicking or tripping
  • Elbowing or aggressive arm use
  • Reckless tackles or dangerous plays
  • Verbal threats, taunting, or provoking
  • Charging the goalkeeper
  • Pulling jerseys or dragging players
